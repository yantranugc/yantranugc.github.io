About me

Why work with me?

I bring something unique — a technical and creative foundation. With a background in tech engineering and an elopement photography business built on raw, documentary-style storytelling, I understand both how products work and how to make them feel human on camera.

As a remote worker and traveler, I shoot not only in cozy homes, but also real destinations. Imagine your brand captured in the mountains, the beaches, or a charming city street. I create scroll-stopping content that positions your brand as part of a lifestyle experience and creates emotional connection. What I film also gives you evergreen assets — timeless visuals you can repurpose across campaigns, seasons, and platforms all year long.

Whether it's paid content built to convert or organic content built to connect — I approach every project with a deep understanding of brand needs and alignment with your social strategy. From storyboarding to video editing, my UGC production spans direct-response ads, relatable day-in-the-life content, and everything in between.

The result? Content that highlights what your product does, feels real to the people watching, builds brand awareness, and converts.
